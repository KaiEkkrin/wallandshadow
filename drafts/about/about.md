# About Wall & Shadow

_Wall & Shadow is a lightweight virtual tabletop for tabletop role-playing games._

## What Wall & Shadow is

Wall & Shadow is a lightweight virtual tabletop (VTT) focused on fast,
improvised battle-map creation for tabletop role-playing games. It is a map
tool — it lets a group draw maps, place tokens, and share what everyone can see,
in real time.

It deliberately does not try to be a full virtual tabletop: there is no dice
roller and no character-sheet system. Groups typically pair it with a Discord
bot or similar for those. Wall & Shadow is aimed at small groups playing
homebrew or improvised games, where the map needs to keep up with whatever the
players decide to do.

## Who runs Wall & Shadow

Wall & Shadow is a small project run by a single individual, Alex Holloway,
based in the United Kingdom. It is a hobby project, not a company.

The service is hosted within the European Union (in Finland), using Hetzner for
hosting and storage.

Because Wall & Shadow is small and solo-run, please be patient with response
times.

## Reporting abuse or illegal content

If you come across content or behaviour on Wall & Shadow that is illegal, or
that breaks the rules in our [Acceptable Use Policy](terms.md#acceptable-use),
please tell us. **You do not need an account to make a report.**

Email **abuse@wallandshadow.com** with:

- a link to, or description of, the content, adventure, or map involved — if you
  can identify it;
- what the problem is; and
- why you believe the content is illegal or breaks the rules.

We review every report. Content that is illegal, or that breaks our rules, is
removed, and we may suspend or close the accounts responsible. If you give us a
return address, we will acknowledge your report as soon as we reasonably can.

**In an emergency** — if someone is in immediate danger — contact your local
emergency services or the police directly. The abuse address is not monitored
around the clock and is not a substitute for emergency services.

## Minimum age

Wall & Shadow is intended for adults. You must be at least 18 years old to
create an account or use the service. See the [Terms of Service](terms.md) and
[Privacy Notice](privacy.md) for details.
